Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a popular American science fiction horror series created by the Duffer Brothers and released on Netflix. The show is set in the 1980s in the small fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, and beautifully blends mystery, nostalgia, and supernatural elements.

The story begins when a young boy named Will Byers mysteriously disappears. As his friends and family search for him, they uncover strange experiments happening at a nearby government lab and encounter a mysterious girl with psychokinetic powers, known only as Eleven. Together, they face horrifying creatures from a dark parallel dimension called the Upside Down.
