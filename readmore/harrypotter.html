Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a world-famous fantasy series written by J.K. Rowling, centered around a young boy who discovers he is a wizard on his 11th birthday. The story follows Harry Potter, an orphan living with his cruel relatives, who learns he’s destined to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

At Hogwarts, Harry befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and together they uncover magical secrets, learn powerful spells, and face dark forces. The main storyline revolves around Harry’s battle against Lord Voldemort, a dark wizard who killed his parents and seeks to dominate the wizarding and non-magical worlds.

Across seven books and eight films, the series explores themes of friendship, bravery, love, sacrifice, and the struggle between good and evil. It’s filled with magical creatures, enchanted objects, and unforgettable characters like Professor Dumbledore, Hagrid, Snape, and Draco Malfoy.
